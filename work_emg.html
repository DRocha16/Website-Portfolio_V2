004 : Electromiography Models For Gesture Prediction

The data

The data for this project was generated using a MYO armband, that has 8 sensors placed in the skin. Four classes of motion were attempted to be predicted by the produced data. The gesture classes were : rock - 0, scissors - 1, paper - 2, ok - 3. These data was recorded at a sampling frequency of 200 Hz, during a record time of 40ms. The dataset was collected from Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/kyr7plus/emg-4

Model Exploration

After the pre-processing of the data, the search for a meaningful model began. Three models were generated using Orange's rapid model testing GUI. With the following results:

- SVM: AUC of 0.605, Precision of 0.723 months and Recall of 0.343

- Neural Network: AUC of 0.995, Precision of 0.959 months and Recall of 0.959

- Naive Bayes : AUC of 0.987, Precision of 0.926 months and Recall of 0.925

So, the best models were the ones using Neural Networks and the ones using the Naive Bayes approach. The train dataset had 6130 labeled examples and the test dataset had 2626 unlabeled examples , a division of 70%/30%.
